1. Who we are

Yellowfone is an AI voice agent built for restaurants. This Privacy Policy describes how Yellowfone ("Yellowfone," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in connection with our website, our voice agent service, and our SMS messaging program (together, the "Service"). The Service is operated by Kyper Technologies.

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account & business information: name, business name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment details when you sign up or request a demo.
  • Communications: messages you send us (email, contact form, SMS), and the call audio and content of calls handled by the voice agent for your business.
  • Customer phone numbers: when a caller dials your Yellowfone number, we receive that caller's phone number (caller ID) along with the call.
  • SMS opt-in: if you or one of your end-customers consents to receive SMS messages from us (for example, an SMS receipt or order update), we collect the mobile number and the opt-in record.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: log files, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps when you use our website or dashboard.
  • Call metadata: date and time of calls, length, routing data, transcripts, and recordings generated by the voice agent.
  • POS & integration data: when you connect a point-of-sale system (e.g., Toast, Square, Clover), we receive menu items, modifiers, prices, and order data necessary to take and submit orders.

3. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service, including answering calls, taking orders, and writing them to your POS.
  • To send transactional messages — SMS receipts, order confirmations, and account/billing notices.
  • To support customers, respond to inquiries, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To analyze aggregated, de-identified data to measure performance and improve our models.
SMS / Text messaging program

4. SMS messaging program disclosures

Yellowfone operates an SMS messaging program used to deliver SMS receipts, order confirmations, account notifications, and (where you have opted in) marketing or product update messages. The following terms apply to all SMS messages sent or received through our program.

We do not share or sell mobile phone numbers. Mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in information, and consent data collected through the SMS messaging program are not shared with, sold to, rented to, or otherwise transferred to any third party for the purposes of marketing, promotion, or independent use. This non-sharing applies to all data obtained through SMS consent and to all categories of information described in this Privacy Policy. The only parties with whom we share such information are service providers strictly necessary to deliver the SMS messages (for example, our messaging carrier and aggregator, such as Twilio), and only for the purpose of delivering those messages on our behalf.

Message frequency. Message frequency varies and depends on your activity with the Service. You may receive up to several messages per order, plus periodic account or billing messages. Transactional message frequency is driven by your use of the Service; marketing messages (if you opt in) are sent no more than a few times per month.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard message and data rates may apply to all SMS messages sent or received through our program, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Yellowfone is not responsible for charges imposed by your carrier.

Opt-out. You can opt out of the SMS messaging program at any time by replying STOP to any message we send you. After you send STOP, we will send you a confirmation message and you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. To rejoin, text START to the same number, or sign up again on our website.

Help. Reply HELP for assistance, or email info@kypertech.com.

Supported carriers. Messaging is supported on major U.S. carriers, including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Boost, Cricket, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

5. How we share information

We share information only as described below, and never sell personal information.

  • Service providers who help us run the Service (hosting, telephony, payments, analytics, customer support). They are contractually limited to using the information only for the services they provide to us.
  • POS & integration partners you have connected to your Yellowfone account (e.g., Toast, Square, Clover), strictly to fulfill orders you have authorized.
  • Legal compliance when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to confidentiality protections.

Mobile information is exempt from third-party sharing. As described in Section 4, no mobile information — including phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, and consent records — is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

6. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, keep you signed in, and measure site usage. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some features of the Service.

7. Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and meet our legal and operational obligations. Call recordings and transcripts are retained for the term of your account; you can export or delete them at any time from the dashboard. Upon cancellation, we delete account data within 30 days unless you request otherwise or we are legally required to retain it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to maintain reasonable protections.

9. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA; residents of other states or countries may have similar rights under applicable law.

To exercise these rights, email info@kypertech.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

10. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.
